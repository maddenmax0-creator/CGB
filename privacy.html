Privacy Policy

CGB Industries · Last updated: July 2026

CGB Industries (“we”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how it is used.

What We Collect

When you submit an enquiry through our contact form, we collect the information you provide: your name, phone number, email address, the service you are enquiring about, and any message details. We do not collect payment information through this website.

How We Use It

We use this information only to respond to your enquiry and provide a quote for the requested service. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Where It's Stored

Form submissions are processed through Basin, a third-party form handling service, and forwarded to our business email. We retain enquiry details only as long as needed to respond to and complete your job.

Third Parties

This site may link to our social media pages (Instagram, Facebook), which have their own privacy policies governing any interaction you have there.

Your Rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you by contacting us at cgbindustries@outlook.com.au.
