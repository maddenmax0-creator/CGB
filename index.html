What We Do

Our Services

Two services. One standard.

Driveways, pool surrounds, paths, and carparks — residential and commercial, anywhere on the Coast.

Pressure Washing

We run rotary surface cleaners, not just a wand — so you get an even finish with no zebra striping. Mould, algae, tyre marks, and years of grime lift in a single visit.

  • Driveways & paths
  • Pool surrounds
  • Commercial surfaces
  • Fencing & retaining walls
Concrete Sealing

Queensland sun and coastal humidity destroy unsealed concrete. We apply clear or colour-enhanced sealers rated for UV and salt exposure.

  • Clear sealing
  • Colour enhancement
  • UV-resistant finishes
  • Anti-slip coatings
